Frequently Asked Questions

What is Relatablez?

Relatablez is a compilation of user-submitted posts starting with the phrase "Am I the only one". We offer users the opportunity to share their thoughts, secrets, fears; you name it, only to discover how similar we truly are.

How Does it Work?

To get started, click "Submit" at the top of the page. Each submission must start with "Am I the only one" followed by your submission. For it to be approved, your submission must go through our moderation process where users decide if it follows our rules. As soon as your submission is online, users are able to start voting using the buttons, "No, me too!" and "You're alone." Users are also able to chat about the submission in the comment section.

Why Should I Sign Up?

Being a member gives you access to interact with the website unlike visitors who are only able to view it. Visitors are only able to view submissions and comments.

Member Benefits:

  • Vote
  • Comment
  • Submit submissions
  • Moderate
  • Customize Your Profile

Why Has My Submission Not Been Approved?

Each submission must go through a moderation process to make sure it follows our guidelines and rules. By clicking "Moderate" at the top of the page, you are able to vote whether a submission meets our rules and guidelines. This process guarantees only high quality content uploaded to Relatablez.

How Can I Find a Specific Submission or Member?

Type keywords, sentences or names into the search bar at the top of the page.
